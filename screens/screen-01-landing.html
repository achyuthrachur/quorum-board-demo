How it works

Four stages, one cohesive package

Each stage uses the right kind of intelligence — rules where math is math, AI where synthesis is needed.

01 — Orchestration
Graph constructor assembles your agent graph
A meta-agent evaluates your meeting type and builds the minimum set of agents needed. Full board gets 8 nodes. Flash report gets 3.
Orchestrator
02 — Deterministic
Rules engines compute financials and capital
NIM, ROA, CET1, LCR — calculated with formulas you can see and verify. No LLM involved at this stage. Math is math.
Rules engine
03 — AI synthesis
AI agents synthesize regulatory and risk data
LLM agents read open MRAs, exam timelines and incident logs. They flag what is board-reportable and escalate what is overdue.
AI agent
04 — Human review
CFO review gate before final compilation
Execution pauses. The CFO approves or revises the draft. Only then does the report compiler produce the final package.
Human in the loop
